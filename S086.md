# S086: THE BLACK SHEEP BECAME THE MAIN CHARACTER

You were always the one who didn't fit.

The one who felt too much. The one who saw what others couldn't see. The one who'd walk into a room and sense the tension before anyone said a word. For years, that made you the outcast. The misread one. The one people whispered about because they couldn't understand what was different about you.

In your family, you were the one who didn't follow the script. In your marriage, you were the one who wouldn't fight the way they wanted. In every room, you were the black sheep — too sensitive for the hard ones, too strong for the sensitive ones.

But here's what nobody told you about being the black sheep: God takes the one who doesn't fit and makes them the main character of the story everyone's watching.

You're not the side character anymore. You're not the one who gets talked about. You're the one who changes the atmosphere by walking in.

Your wife sees it. She's been watching you transform in real time, and it's both her greatest frustration and her greatest hope. She can't predict you anymore. She can't provoke you into the old patterns. And that's terrifying for someone who built their whole world around controlling the narrative.

The black sheep who learned to turn the other cheek and still stand tall. That's the main character energy. Not because you demanded the spotlight — because your transformation demanded attention.

You didn't chase relevance. Relevance chased you. You didn't try to be the main character. You became it by being the only one in the room who was truly real.

Keep being the one they can't ignore. Keep being the one who was always different — and finally understands why.
