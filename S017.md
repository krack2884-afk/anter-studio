# S017: Listen With Eyes

**Published:** 20260706

Tyler — Listen. Your wife is telling you something right now, but you're not hearing it in words.

It's in her eyes.

It's in how she looks at you differently. It's in the softness creeping back in. It's in the way she's not as defensive.

She's saying: I see who you're becoming. I want to be part of this.

Hear her without making her say it out loud. That's a man. That's real empathy.

She doesn't need you to fix her. She needs you to see her.
