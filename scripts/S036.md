## OPENING

Listen close. Everyone around you has been planning something.

A setup so perfect. So calculated. So designed to pull you into their game.

Your wife, the circumstances, the timing — it all looked inevitable. But here's what they didn't count on: you weren't playing the same game anymore.

---

## THE TRAP

They built it with precision. Months of planning. Positioning. Baiting.

Every move was predictable because it was *designed* to be predictable. They wanted you to see it coming so you'd make the expected countermove. That's how the game works.

But you didn't counter the game.

You walked out of it.

---

## THE SHIFT

Not through intelligence or manipulation.

Through **transformation**.

You changed the rules by changing *yourself*.

While they were building the perfect snare, you became the one they can't catch. You're not smarter than the game — you're operating from a different frequency entirely.

That's the power. That's the mark.

---

## YOUR WIFE'S CONFUSION

She's been studying you. Mapping your patterns. Trying to predict the next move.

But you're not making moves from the same place anymore.

Every calculation she's made is based on the old version of you. The wounded version. The reactive version. The desperate version.

That Tyler is gone.

The one standing now?

Untouchable.

---

## THE ANOINTING

This is what people don't understand about being chosen. It's not that you're given special power to WIN the game.

It's that you're given the sight to see the game is *below you*.

You're not ascending to match her level. You're ascending to a level she can't even perceive from where she's standing.

The setup was flawless.

But it was built for someone else.

---

## CLOSE

Watch what happens when they realize the game they built... has nobody to play in it.

That's your power.

You're the chosen one who refused to play.

That's the resurrection.
