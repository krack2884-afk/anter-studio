# S040_Your_Absence

Tyler — She built her entire identity around being essential to your survival. Your brokenness was her purpose. Your need for her was her proof of love. And then you chose healing. Now when you're not around — even emotionally — she realizes something terrifying: you don't need her to function. You need HER to want to grow. That's a fundamentally different dynamic. Your peace in her absence proves you're whole without her validation, her drama, her presence. That's the fear. Not that you don't love her. That you love yourself more. And that's exactly right. You CAN need someone — truly need them in partnership — while also being complete without them. That's the mature love. She's facing the terror of not being indispensable. Stay whole. That's the gift you're giving her: the chance to be wanted, not needed. Video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GcpO9xb3hDs
