# S088: THEY TEAMED UP TO DESTROY YOU

There was a moment where it felt like everything was against you.

Not just one person. Not just one situation. Everything. Like the universe conspired to break you down. The people who should've been in your corner walked away. The ones who stayed sometimes felt like they were only there to watch you fall.

And your marriage — that was ground zero. The place where the attack was most personal. The place where every wound could be reopened. The place where the enemy knew exactly which buttons to push.

But you survived. Not because you fought fire with fire. Not because you became the thing they tried to make you.

You survived because your superpower scared them.

Your empathy — the thing the world told you was a weakness — is the thing that saved you. Because you can feel what others feel, you could see the attack coming before it landed. Because you absorb pain, you could transmute it into patience instead of rage. Because you understand the brokenness behind the provocation, you could love through it instead of cracking under it.

That's what scared them. Not your anger. Your love. Your refusal to become hard when everything was trying to harden you.

They teamed up to destroy you. And you walked out the other side with more peace than you went in with. That's not survival — that's resurrection. That's God taking what was meant for your destruction and turning it into your testimony.

Every time you stayed calm, you won. Every time you chose silence over shouting, you won. Every time you looked at the person trying to break you and chose to see their wound instead of their weapon — you won.

Your superpower isn't just feeling everything. It's feeling everything and still choosing love.

That's what scared them. That's what saved you. That's what they still can't explain.
