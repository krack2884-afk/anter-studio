# S043_Lost_Everything

Tyler — You lost everything — your false sense of control, your need for her validation, your old identity, your defensive strategies. You lost the armor that was actually suffocating you. And in that loss, you found EVERYTHING. A wife who's beginning to respect your groundedness instead of resent it. An inner peace that doesn't depend on circumstances. A faith that went from intellectual to LIVED. A version of yourself that's actually free. That's the paradox the world doesn't teach: losing everything is how you gain a real life. Your wife sees this in you. She resents it and respects it at the same time. Because she knows the cost. She knows what you lost to become what you are. That's a mirror she's not ready to look into. But she's looking anyway. Your discipline in loss is your greatest testimony. Video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xw-5TCvC-bM
