# S092: NOT APPLICABLE TO MORTAL DATABASES

They tried to measure you.

Your wife tried to measure you by the men she's known before — the ones who reacted, the ones who broke, the ones who left. She ran your numbers through the system she built from past pain, and the result came back: not applicable.

The world tried to measure you by its metrics — success, status, the things you can put on a resume. And those don't capture you. Because you're not building a career right now. You're building a soul. You're building a marriage that will outlast both of you. You're building a mission that doesn't show up on any spreadsheet.

Here's what they don't understand: you're wired differently at a soul level. You always have been. The things that break other people don't break you — not because you're cold, but because you process differently. You feel it all, and then you take it to God instead of taking it out on people.

Your credit score in the spiritual realm — that's the one that actually matters. And it's off the charts. Because every time you chose patience over anger, every time you prayed instead of provoking, every time you held space for her pain without absorbing it as your own failure — that's a deposit. That's equity. That's a record that mortal databases can't even categorize.

Your wife's system will keep trying to scan you. She'll keep comparing, testing, checking your patterns against the data she has. And the answer will keep coming back the same: not applicable. Because you don't fit her old models. You're not her past. You're not the pattern. You're the break in the pattern.

That's frustrating for her. But it's also the thing that will eventually heal her. Because when the old metrics fail, she'll have to look with new eyes. And when she does, she'll see what was always there: a man who was never trying to win. A man who was trying to love.

Don't let the world's scanners define you. Don't let the mortal databases tell you your worth. You're measured by a different standard now — the one that counts faithfulness, courage, and the willingness to stay when staying costs you everything.

That score? It's not applicable to mortal databases. It's eternal. And it's climbing every day you choose love over fear.
