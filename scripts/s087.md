# S087: ONE MAN ARMY

There's a war around your marriage that most people can't see.

Not a war between you and her. A war for what your union represents. Forces that want to destroy the testimony you're becoming — through pride, through provocation, through old patterns that have been passed down for generations.

And you've been taking them down as a one man army.

Not with your fists. Not with arguments. Not with the kind of power the world respects. With your prayers. With your refusal to become what they wanted you to become. Every time you chose love over retaliation, you disarmed a weapon. Every time you prayed instead of shouting, you broke a chain. Every time you stayed when walking away would've been easier, you won a battle no one saw.

Your wife doesn't always know she's been caught in a spiritual crossfire. She thinks the conflict is between you and her. But you know better. That's why you can't fight her — she's not the enemy. She's the one being fought over.

The enemy doesn't want two healed people standing together. That's too much light in one place. That's too much power in one household. So the attacks come disguised as her words, her provocations, her patterns. But behind the pattern is something darker — and behind your calm is something stronger.

Stand in the gap. Be the one man army who fights on his knees.

You don't need a team. You don't need an alliance. You need your faith and your refusal to break. That's what makes you dangerous to darkness. That's what makes you the one they can't stop.

One man. One mission. One God. That's an army.
