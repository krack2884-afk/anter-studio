# S013: Kindness Ripples

**Published:** 20260630

Tyler — Your kindness ripples further than you know.

An empath's strength isn't in retaliation. It's not in being strong in the way the world defines strength.

It's in healing. In grace. In showing up as whole.

Every act of grace from you creates a spiritual debt in the universe. People feel that. They remember it. They come back asking for more of what you have.

That's real power.

Not force. Magnetism.
